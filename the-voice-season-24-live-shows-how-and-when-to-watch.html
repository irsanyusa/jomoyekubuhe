'The Voice' Season 24 Live ShowsHow and When to Watch

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, May 23, 2024

Photo by: Tyler Golden/NBC

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Coaches on The Voice have finally chosen the 12 singers who are advancing to the Live Shows. As we take a step closer to the Finale, it’s best to know everything about the upcoming round. Here are the details we have so far.

When and Where to Watch The Voice Season 24 Live Shows

Ahead of the Live Shows, coaches Reba McEntire, Gwen Stefani, Niall Horan, and John Legend decided who would be moving forward in the competition. These artists are undeniably the best among each team, surviving the Playoffs, the Knockouts, the Battle Rounds, and the Blind Auditions.

For Team Legend, Azán, Lila Forde, and Mac Royals will compete. Meanwhile, fans will see Team Niall fight with Huntley, Nini Iris, and Mara Justine. For Team Gwen, Bias, Tanner Massey, and Kara Tenae will perform in the Live Shows. Lastly, for Team Reba, Ruby Leigh, Jordan Rainer, and Jacquie Roar will belt their hearts out.

The Live Shows begin on Monday, December 4, 8/7c on NBC, next day on Peacock. Top 12 Results air live Tuesday, December 5 at 9/8c on NBC and stream next day on Peacock.

The Semi-Finals will happen in the subsequent week, starting Monday, December 11, and the Results for the Semi-Finals air Tuesday, December 12.

For the Top 9, four artists will be voted into the Finale, with the bottom five set to compete for the Instant Save. Whoever artist that wins will go straight to the Finals. This means the December 18 Finale will feature five artists.

The Voice season 24 Finale performances air Monday, December 18 at 8/7c on NBC and next day on Peacock. Finally, the Finale Live Results will take place on December 19 at 9/8c and goes to 11/10c.

How to Vote for Your Favorite Artist?

One of the many exciting things about the Live Shows is that fans can have the power to vote for their favorite artists weekly. As soon as the Live Show begins, artists will perform each week and from there, fans can vote on who they want to stay.

For The Voice season 24, fans get one vote per email address per method. Fans can either vote on the website or The Voice Official App. In addition, fans will be able to vote after the December 4 episode (Top 12 Artists), December 11 episode (Top 9 Artists), and December 18 episode (Top 5 Artists).

The voting window is 6:55 p.m. PT/9:55 p.m. ET to 4 a.m. PT after the above episodes.

If you want to know who among the 12 artists will make it to the Finale, be sure to tune in on The Voice on Mondays and Tuesdays at 8/7c on NBC.
