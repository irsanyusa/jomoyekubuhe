Dancing with the Stars fans think there might be a romance brewing between Season 32 contestant Harry Jowsey and his partner Rylee Arnold. Reactions to the potential relationship on social media are mixed, with some loving the idea and others expressing discomfort.

Harry Jowsey, Rylee Arnold Spark Romance Rumors

Jowsey is a reality star and influencer. For Season 32 of DWTS, he’s paired with first-time pro Rylee Arnold, who is the younger sister of former pro Lindsay Arnold. Earlier this week, Jowsey gushed about his partner in an interview with Us Weekly.

“She’s never in a bad mood and she’s always bubbly and brings me up when I’m feeling a little bit flat, or in my head about things, and knows when to sit me down and talk me out of whatever hump I’m in,” Jowsey shared. “I feel like everyone needs that in a person in their life. She’s everything you could ever dream of.”

Jowsey also had some interesting things to say about his love life, sharing that it’s “to be decided” whether he’s single, as he added, “Honestly, I’m just focused on Rylee right now.”

Some commenters on Us Weekly‘s Instagram post about the interview seemed to think Jowsey’s comments could hint at a romance with Arnold. One user pointed out their age difference, as Jowsey is 26 and Arnold is 18. Another wrote, “Protect her from him please.”

Fans React to the Pair Holding Hands

As if Jowsey’s comments weren’t enough to fuel dating rumors, he and Arnold were recently spotted holding hands at the Hollywood Palladium. In a video posted to TikTok, they can be seen hand in hand as they walk through a crowd of people.

Several commenters wrote that they predicted the pair would end up dating. One simply wrote, “I hope this is real.” However, others thought it might just be a friendly gesture, as they tried not to get separated in the crowd.

Last month, Lindsay Arnold commented on her sister’s partnership with Jowsey on the Weekly Trash podcast, saying they “look so hot” in their promo photos but jokingly warning Jowsey, “Don’t kiss my sister.”
