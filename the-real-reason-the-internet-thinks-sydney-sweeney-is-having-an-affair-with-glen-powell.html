The internet is currently abuzz with rumors of an alleged affair between Glen Powell and Sydney Sweeney, co-stars in an upcoming rom-com film directed by William Puck. Fans fueled the rumors further by speculating that the undeniable chemistry between the two actors, seen in behind-the-scenes clips and photos, could only be the result of method acting.

Sydney Sweeney is Rumored to Have an Affair With Glen Powell

The rumors of a real-life relationship between Powell and Sweeney have taken the internet by storm. Fans of the two have been sharing clips and photos from the set of their upcoming rom-com. Each photo and video seems to suggest that the two have tons of chemistry on and off screen.

Powell’s rumored split with his long-time girlfriend, Gigi Paris, and Sweeney’s engagement to entrepreneur Jonathan Davino last year have made their rumored affair even more intriguing. While the rumors of cheating are unsubstantiated, they have undoubtedly caused a frenzy on social media.

The rumors of cheating are, of course, problematic and can cause real harm to the parties involved. However, it’s also worth noting that the internet’s fascination with celebrity relationships and scandals is nothing new.

In the end, the rumored affair between Powell and Sweeney is just that, a rumor. It’s important not to jump to conclusions or make assumptions about their personal lives. Whether or not the rumors are true, there’s no denying that Powell and Sweeney are a captivating pair on screen, and their rumored off-screen romance only adds to excitement. Since many can say they see their off-screen chemistry, many are looking forward to the film’s premiere as a result.

Sydney Sweeney Got Unfollowed By Powell’s Girlfriend Gigi Paris

Ever since rumors of Sweeney and Powell have skyrocketed to popularity, it’s been reported that Powell’s girlfriend, Paris, as unfollowed Sweeney on Instagram. Many think that this could mean Powell and Paris have called it quits in the recent weeks. This came after Sweeney and Powell have been filming together in Australia for the upcoming rom-com.

Initially, Paris showed her support for Sweeney with the casting, leaving celebratory comments on on the actress’s Instagram posts. However, rumors suggest that Paris’ relationship with Powell has been on the rocks since they began filming the movie in Sydney, Australia. Paris traveled to be with Powell on set in late March, but returned to the US less than two weeks later.

This unfollowing incident has caused fans to speculate about the relationship status between Powell and Paris, and whether Sweeney’s involvement in the rom-com has played a part in their alleged breakup.

The untitled rom-com also stars Bryan Brown, Michelle Hurd, and Darren Barnet. Paparazzi cameras have already captured PDA-packed moments between Powell and Sweeney on set, including photos of them stripping down to their bathing suits and making out on camera.
