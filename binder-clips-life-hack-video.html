15 Ways to Use Binder Clips: Life Hack Video

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, August 10, 2024

January 29, 2016 12:02 PM EST

You probably have a couple binder clips laying in the bottom of a drawer somewhere. You should probably go find them right now.

This video, from the Japanese lifehack YouTube channel Venlee, demonstrates 15 creative ways to use those clips to organize cables, store snacks, knit and more. It’s a mesmerizing four minutes that will leave you feeling like you can be in control of your life. You can have it all. All you need is a handful of binder clips.

Some of this YouTube channel’s other lifehack videos include how to fold a T-shirt in two seconds and how to make the perfect poached egg.
