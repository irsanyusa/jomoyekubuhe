America’s Got Talent star Peter Rosalita recently performed his first-ever solo concert in Dubai, and the entire show is available to watch on YouTube. The young Filipino singer made it to the Semifinals of Season 16 last year at age 10.

Peter Rosalita Performs First Solo Concert in Dubai

Peter performed his concert earlier this month at La Mer in Dubai as part of the city’s Shopping Festival. The singer, now 11, hit the stage for two nights in a row. He performed covers of songs such as “I’ll Be There” by the Jackson 5 and “Talking to the Moon” by Bruno Mars.

He even sang an epic rendition of “Bohemian Rhapsody” by Queen, as well as “Dynamite” by BTS. He was accompanied on stage by a band of young instrumentalists called Cadenza Boy Band.

Peter also shared a few inspiring messages with the crowd throughout the concert. Before a performance of “Stand Up for Love” by Destiny’s Child, he said, “If we stand up for love, we will all succeed.” He finished the concert by singing “When I Look at You” by Miley Cyrus.

What Else Has Peter Been Up to Since ‘AGT’?

After competing on America’s Got Talent, Peter returned home to Abu Dhabi, but he’s also traveled to Los Angeles in the past few months. He even made an appearance on The Ellen DeGeneres Show, performing “Never Enough” from The Greatest Showman.

Peter also recently celebrated his 11th birthday. He showed off his cake and balloons on Instagram and thanked his fans for sending him birthday messages. In December, he performed “O Holy Night” at a Christmas event in Dubai.

“My dreams are always close to my heart and I hope that 2022 will open up more doors of opportunity for me to grow as an artist and propel my career further,” Peter said in a recent interview.
