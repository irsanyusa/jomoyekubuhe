Test: Can You Find the 5Words Hidden inthe Picture?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, July 10, 2024

A great way to train your attention and focus is by solving puzzles and riddles. Even our distant ancestors knew it — some of the earliest known riddles date back to Babylonian times! So if you want to put your ability to notice small details to the test, you’ve come to the right place.

Here at Bright Side, we’ve created a riddle for your eyes. Let’s do this quick brain workout!

We’ve hidden 5 words in this picture. Can you find them?

1. Couch

2. Lamp

3. Plant

4. Pet

5. Carpet

How many words did you find? Did you like this visual puzzle? Please let us know in the comments below.
