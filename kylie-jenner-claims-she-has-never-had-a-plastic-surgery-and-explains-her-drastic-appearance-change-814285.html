Kylie Jenner Claims She Has Never Had a Plastic Surgery And Explains Her Drastic Appearance Ch

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, July 15, 2024

Kylie Jenner has managed to turn her appearance into a successful makeup empire. There have been speculations about whether she’s had any cosmetic procedures. Still, in a recent interview, the reality TV star addresses these rumors and honestly shares what she has or hasn’t done to her face.

Kylie Jenner clarified she isn’t as plastic as people think.

During a confessional segment, Kylie Jenner addressed misconceptions about her and plastic surgery. She shared that contrary to popular assumptions, she has always been confident and has not undergone extensive procedures to change her entire face. She clarified that she only got fillers and emphasized that she didn’t want that to define her story.

In a previous interview, she also mentioned how she feels about cosmetic surgery. “People think I fully went under the knife and completely reconstructed my face, which is completely false. I’m terrified! I would never.”

After Kylie Jenner’s statement about not having plastic surgery on her face, critics have responded with trolling comments and jokes, doubting the possibility of her not having any procedures at all. However, it’s important to consider the context of her words. Kylie specifically denied facial plastic surgery while maintaining that other potential procedures elsewhere are her personal business and shouldn’t be a topic of public discussion.

She did admit to having lip fillers when she was younger.

Kylie Jenner’s distinctive pouty lips, which have become her trademark, were not something she was born with. In her own words, she revealed that while she was a confident child overall, she had one major insecurity: her lips. Over time, she decided to address this concern, and her decision to get lip fillers eventually became public knowledge.

It took her a while to openly speak about it, but she admitted to getting temporary lip fillers in a 2015 episode of Keeping Up With the Kardashians. “It was the best thing I’ve ever done. I don’t regret it. But I always thought I was cute.” In 2018 however, Jenner revealed she’s gotten rid of all her fillers.

Jenner also shared that motherhood has made her feel more beautiful.

Kylie Jenner, a proud mother to 5-year-old Stormi and 1-year-old Aire, shared that motherhood has had a profound impact on her self-perception. Having children has given her a newfound appreciation for her unique features. She expressed how seeing her traits reflected in her daughter and son has deepened her love for herself.
