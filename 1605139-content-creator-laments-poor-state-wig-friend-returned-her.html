Content Creator Laments Poor State of Wig Friend Returned to Her, Peeps React: &quot;She Is Ungrateful&quot;

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, July 17, 2024
  • A content creator, Precious, gave out her expensive wig to one of her friends, who borrowed it for a photoshoot session
  • When it was time to return the wig, her fried kept postponing it till it got to one month, and she returned it in a bad state
  • Precious said she was destabilised over the current state of the wig, but her friend was not even remorseful about how she returned it

Content creator Precious Ubani got the attention of netizens after she shared the bad state her friend returned the wig she borrowed from her a month ago.

She noted that the curly human hair cost her N500k, and she used it for her birthday, which led to her friend requesting it.

Her friend wanted to use it for a photoshoot, and she obliged. However, she took a while to return it, and it was in a terrible state.

Precious said she was confused about what to do as she displayed the damaged wig. Some netizens stated that the lady changed Precious' wig while others gave their take on what she should do.
