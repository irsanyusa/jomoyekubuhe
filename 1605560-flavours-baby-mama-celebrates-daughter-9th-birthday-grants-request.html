Flavour's Baby Mama Celebrates Their Daughter On 9th Birthday, Grants Her Request: &quot;Mummy's Pride&quot;

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, July 26, 2024
  • Singer Flavour's baby mama, Anna Ebiere Banner, was filled with love and happiness as she celebrated her daughter Sofia's birthday
  • She posted her picture of her daughter, who she described as her precious jewel, and how she met her birthday request
  • The former beauty queen noted that her daughter was not so little anymore, and she got several birthday wishes from her fans

Anna Ebiere Banner, the baby mama of singer Chinedu Okoli, aka Flavour, celebrated her daughter Sophia's ninth birthday on August 1.

Anna, who won the Most Beautiful Girl in Nigeria (MBGN) in 2013, said her child was not as little as she used to be.

She also described her as her pride and precious jewel. Sophia wanted a photoshoot inspired by the film character Wednesday Addams, and her mother granted her request.

The birthday girl rocked a black dress with white flowery designs and was styled with a white collar and matching white fabric at the hem of her wrists.

She combined her attire with a stylish fringe hairstyle and kept a straight face for her photoshoot session.

Friends and fans of the former beauty queen joined her to celebrate her daughter's birthday on her Instagram page.
