For me, MI Abaga is the number 1 rapper in Africa - SA rapper Nasty C

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, August 7, 2024

- Nasty C recently left social media buzzing after he revealed MI Abaga is his number 1 rapper in Africa

- The South African rapper who recently got signed to Def Jam Records, revealed this in an Instagram live interview

- The post has caused quite a buzz as Nasty C is believed to be Africa's best rapper, not MI

South African top rapper, David Junior Ngcobo professionally known as Nasty C, has declared MI Abaga as the best rapper in Africa and fans are surprised.

The talented rapper revealed this in a recent Instagram live video, after he was asked who his top 5 rappers in Africa were.

Nasty C who recently got signed to Def Jam Records, stated that he didn't know a lot of rappers but his number one rapper in Africa is MI Abaga.

Holding a different opinion is Nigerian rapper Yung6ix, as he appears to be holding a grudge against MI Abaga and this was recently made public via a post shared on his timeline on Twitter.
