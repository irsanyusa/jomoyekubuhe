Danielle Steel (Novelist) - On This Day

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, July 26, 2024

Profession: Novelist

Biography: Danielle Steel is one of the most popular authors in the United States, specializing in romance novels. She has written more than 190 books, many of which have been bestsellers.

Steel's work primarily focuses on real-life situations, such as illness, death, and the human condition, which are typically woven into dramatic narratives. She has been noted for her consistent productivity, often publishing several books in a single year. Several of her novels have also been adapted into television movies, including The Promise (1979), Kaleidoscope (1990), The Gift (1994), and The House (2015).

Despite her already prolific career, Steel continues to write and publish new work, maintaining a rigorous writing schedule. Her novels continue to be popular worldwide, and her overall book sales exceed 800 million copies.
