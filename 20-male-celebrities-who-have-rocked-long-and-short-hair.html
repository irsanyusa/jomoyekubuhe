20 Male Celebrities Who Have Rocked Long and Short Hair

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, July 22, 2024

Jared Leto Long hair: A man of many looks, Jared Leto has experimented with various hair lengths in his career. From 2012 to current times, Leto has rocked longer locks , aside from cutting his hair for his role as The Joker in Suicide Squad. Short hair: Leto has always favored a longer mop, even in his My So-Called Life days. For film roles, however, the actor and 30 Seconds to Mars singer has cut his hair, like in 2017 for his comic-inspired role as The Joker. David Crotty - Getty Images

Jason Momoa Long hair: Jason Momoa has long, luscious locks that would make anyone jealous. While he has said his current hairstyle is inspired by his ongoing role as Aquaman, Momoa has had long hair for most of his career long before his superhero days. Short hair: While its hard to imagine Momoa with a short hairstyle, the actor rocked a cropped do in 2013. He quickly went back to his long roots after the chop, flawlessly serving every hair length in between during the grow out process. Getty Images
