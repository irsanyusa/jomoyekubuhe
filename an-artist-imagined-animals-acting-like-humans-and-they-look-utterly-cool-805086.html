An Artist Imagined Animals Acting Like Humans and They Look Utterly Cool

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, July 12, 2024

Sometimes, when life gets too boring and repetitive, all we need to do is see things from a different perspective. This is exactly what Taiwanese American artist, Lucia Heffernan, does with her artwork. On her Instagram account, Lucia showcases her unique paintings that portray cute and cuddly animals in typical human situations.

To put a little smile on your face today, Bright Side has curated some of her most relatable creations. See if the last photo reminds you of someone!

A sea lion teaching us about body positivity

Even owls have late night thoughts

What do you call a high-fashion feline? Haute Cat-ure!

How much would you tip this pizza delivery pup?

She’s a yogi chick

The latest fitness craze: Pig-lates

A howlin’ good time, indeed!

“Does this shade make my snout pop?”

This gym rat has an axe to grind

A typical day in a mama dog’s life...

Too bad those headphones don’t fit his droopy ears

A bunny going on her first date

A purr-fect weekend

She’s a unicorn and no one can tell her otherwise!

Ahh, animals. They’re really just like us. Which of these furballs can you relate to the most? Sound off your thoughts in the comments!
