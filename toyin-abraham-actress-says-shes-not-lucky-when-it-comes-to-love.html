Actress says she's not lucky when it comes to love

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, July 21, 2024

The star was a guest on a new episode of Rubbin Minds with Ebuka Obi-Uchendu where she revealed to Ebuka that she regrets ever going into a relationship with her ex, Seun Egbegbe.

Abraham who was close to tears explained that it was a very emotional period for her and as such, she always empathises with women going through similar emotional stress.

She also revealed she hasn't always been lucky when it comes to love.

"I'm the kind of girl I want to cook for my man. But no it doesn't work. So. I'm just gonna be me now.

For now, it's my career. I'm married to my work. I don't think I'm lucky with love but I'm lucky with my job."

Meanwhile, she also revealed she kind of changed her name because she wants to start afresh. "It's a new beginning. New me, new start."

On her relationship with Seun Egbegbe, "I regret the relationship with Seun. I made a terrible mistake."
