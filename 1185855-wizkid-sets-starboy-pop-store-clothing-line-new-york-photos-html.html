Wizkid sets up StarBoy pop-up store for his clothing line in New York (photos)

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, August 17, 2024

Nigerian singer Ayo Balogun, popularly known as Wizkid, has opened a pop-up store for his clothing line in New York City. The singer announced the exciting news on social media, revealing the launch date.

The singer who is also known as StarBoy named the store after himself and his StarBoy clothing collection.

StarBoy pop-store launched on Saturday, August 11. The singer who announced the news on his Instagram page, revealed that he would be at the store to meet fans from 12pm to 7pm.

Wizkid also shared photos from the store which was already filled up with items for fans to purchase at the launched.
