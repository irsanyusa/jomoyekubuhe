Elizabeth Hurley, 58, Reveals Secret Childhood Skill That Keeps Her Fit and Wow-Worthy

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, August 15, 2024

When you scroll through the photos on Elizabeth Hurley’s Instagram, you may immediately start wondering what the actress does to keep her body and skin looking so fantastic. The 58-year-old British actress and model manages to look half her age. Her skin glows, and her body looks fabulous in a bikini. She shared her secrets that help her look younger, and we are prepared to take notes.

Elizabeth gets people talking with her Instagram photos.

Anyone who visits Elizabeth Hurley’s Instagram might think her whole life is just one extended, sun-kissed holiday. The page blossoms with photos of the actress being in sunlight, showing off her enviable body in various exotic locations.

But the 58-year-old revealed that the secret to her glowing skin is, in fact, keeping out of the sun.

While sharing a photo of herself bathing in the sun, she wrote, “LA morning wake up. I only go in the sun before 9am and after 6pm — otherwise, it’s under a large umbrella.”

Speaking about how she stays in such good shape, Elizabeth explained she “makes time for exercise” and eats “nutritious foods”.

Elizabeth insists on mild and soft approach to fitness routine.

The actress doesn’t follow hard and fast rules. She simply does everything to support her fit and healthy lifestyle. According to her recommendations, no super long hours at the gym are necessary to look like a goddess.

The actress, in fact, is not a gym person at all. She genuinely admits that she actually avoids the gym when she can.

Instead, the actress advocates the powers of so-called found fitness. She simply finds ways to stay fit in her daily routine. In her interview, she said, “I don’t really do any set exercise, per se, because I prefer to get my exercise from doing something than being in the gym.”

For Elizabeth, building up a sweat through any of her regular actions is much more useful than a gym machine or even a treadmill.

Elizabeth has her own childhood skill that helps her stay fit.

In one of her interviews, Elizabeth gave a rare insight into her childhood. She revealed the lifelong hobby that helps her stay in marvelous shape. The actress showed a photo of a cycling proficiency badge, which she received in the 1970s. This proves the global icon has always been into cycling — so that’s how she keeps her stellar body in a wonderful shape.
