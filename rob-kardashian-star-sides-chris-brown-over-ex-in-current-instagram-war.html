Star Sides Chris Brown Over Ex In Current Instagram War

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, June 23, 2024

Chris lashed out on Instagram yesterday calling Adrienne a "trout mouth ass b***h" and Tamar a "muppet face a** b**ch". While some were quick to defend her, Adrienne's ex, Rob, 27, showed his support for Chris, 25.

Chris expressed his obvious dislike for Adrienne – who once dated Rob back in 2009 – for trying to dish out relationship advice to Karrueche after allegedly 'dating married men'. He posted Adrienne's photo with a nasty caption Nov. 3.

Well to show which side he's on, Rob liked the photo ...or lets say the post Breezy put up slamming 'The Real' co-host. He also followed the "Loyal" singer on Instagram! It seems the The Keeping Up With The Kardashians stars still has bitter feelings towards his ex.
