5 Must-visit gyms in Lagos

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, July 2, 2024

1.i-Fitness gym

i-Fitness gym is one of the most widely spread gyms in Nigeria. It has over 15 branches in Lagos only. They provide a wide range of different sessions or classes that you can sign up for.

2.Bodyline fitness gym

ADVERTISEMENT

It’s also one of the leading centres in the fitness world, with qualified training personnel and sophisticated training equipment. It’s located in Ikoyi, quite accessible to those who live on the island.

3.Elitebox fitness

Elitebox fitness is a very accessible gym to those on the mainland, especially to guests lodged in Eko Hotel as they’re in the same proximity. They have a guideline on which gym membership to apply for, which consists of the possibilities and your availability in coming to the gym.

4.Maxout fitness

This gym is located in Ketu, you can also shop for fitness accessories like knee pads, and many more. It's open most times of the day when everyone will be active, so it's a comfortable and safe place to visit.

5.Fitnigeria

It's located in Lekki and they're open from Mondays to Saturdays. However, they provide a service where they can help build your home gym. All you have to do is contact them and state all you want to get.

We hope you’ve checked out these gyms on Google already and you’ve found the one you’re so sure you’ll visit. Just make sure you get all your equipment and accessories ready and find which plan is best for you. It's time to get to the gym!
