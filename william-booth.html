William Booth (Co-founder of the Salvation Army)

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, July 29, 2024

Profession: Co-founder of the Salvation Army

Biography: Co-founded the Salvation Army in 1865 with his wife Catherine Booth.

Born: April 10, 1829
Birthplace: Sneinton, Nottingham, England, United Kingdom
Star Sign: Aries

Died: August 20, 1912 (aged 83)
