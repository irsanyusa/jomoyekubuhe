Ranking The Top 10 Best Paula Abdul Songs Ever

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, July 24, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Photo by Harmony Gerber/Getty Images

Reading Time: 3 minutes

Paula Abdul has been an iconic figure in the world of music and dance for decades. Throughout her career, she has enchanted fans with her mesmerizing dance moves, captivating voice, and timeless pop hits. As Abdul prepares to grace the stage as a guest judge on the upcoming week 7 episode of DWTS Season 32, let’s take a trip down memory lane and celebrate her illustrious career by counting down the top 10 best Paula Abdul songs ever.

Ranking The Top 10 Best Paula Abdul Songs Ever

10. “My Love Is for Real”

With Middle Eastern influences and a mesmerizing beat, “My Love Is for Real” is a unique and exotic addition to Abdul’s discography, proving her willingness to experiment with different musical styles.

9. “Will You Marry Me?”

This touching ballad, featured on her album Spellbound, demonstrated Abdul’s ability to tug at the heartstrings while showcasing her heartfelt lyrics and emotional depth.

8. “Vibeology”

“Vibeology” brought a fresh, funky sound to Abdul’s repertoire, blending elements of new jack swing and pop. It remains a hit that gets people moving to this day.

7. “Knocked Out”

“Knocked Out” was one of Abdul‘s early hits and served as a sneak peek into her promising career. Both its catchy melody and confident vocals made it an instant fan favorite.

6. “The Way That You Love Me”

“The Way That You Love Me” is a high-energy pop track with a catchy hook, showcasing both Abdul’s irresistible charisma and dance sensibilities.

5. “Forever Your Girl”

The title track from her debut album, “Forever Your Girl” is a heartfelt song that resonated with fans and also established Abdul as a force to be reckoned with in the music industry.

4. “Rush, Rush”

Abdul’s sultry ballad “Rush, Rush” demonstrated her versatility as an artist. It also became a timeless classic and showcased her vocal range and emotional depth.

3. “Opposites Attract”

Featuring the animated MC Skat Kat, “Opposites Attract” showcases Abdul’s signature blend of both pop and R&B. Both the catchy tune and playful music video made it a standout hit.

2. “Straight Up”

“Straight Up” is the song that certainly catapulted Abdul to stardom. This infectious pop track dominated the charts in the late 1980s and is still a dancefloor favorite today.

1. “Cold Hearted”

With its bold choreography and bold lyrics, “Cold Hearted” was a rebellious anthem that further solidified Abdul’s status as a pop icon.
