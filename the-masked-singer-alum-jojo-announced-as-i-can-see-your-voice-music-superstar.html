I Can See Your Voice is airing a special episode this Sunday, with singer JoJo as the Music Superstar. JoJo was the runner-up on The Masked Singer Season 5, disguised as Black Swan. This Sunday’s episode will be a reunion with judge Ken Jeong, who hosts ICSYV.

JoJo to Appear on I Can See Your Voice

Last week, I Can See Your Voice announced that it would be airing a special episode on Sunday, June 26, at 7/6c on FOX. Fans speculated that it was a previously unaired episode from Season 2, which ended in March. The new announcement about JoJo confirms this.

FOX previously released photos from “Episode 11,” featuring guest panelists Drew Carey, Yvette Nicole Brown, and musical guest JoJo. Permanent panelists Cheryl Hines and Adrienne Houghton will also appear, along with host Ken Jeong.

The show also revealed this week’s contestant on Instagram. Dr. Dossier Harps will be trying to figure out which singers are good and bad in Sunday’s episode, with the help of the celebrity panel.

How Does ICSYV Work?

ICSYV is a music talent show in which a contestant is tasked with identifying good and bad singers without ever hearing the people sing. They can win up to $100,000 by correctly guessing whether the last singer is good or bad, as revealed in a duet with that week’s Music Superstar.

Depending on whether the singer is actually good, this duet might sound amazing, or it could crash and burn. We’re excited to see JoJo perform with the final singer on this Sunday’s new episode.

The “Leave (Get Out)” singer recently finished her 2022 tour, and also appeared in a recurring role on the CW series All American, playing a recording artist named Sabine. Last year, she released the album Trying Not to Think About It.
